About MCAMS

The Medical Center Appointments Management System (MCAMS) is a comprehensive web-based solution designed to streamline healthcare appointments.

Developed by students at Cairo University's Faculty of Graduate Studies for Statistical Research, Department of Computer Science, MCAMS addresses the challenges of appointment scheduling in medical centers.

Our system provides an efficient platform for patients to book appointments, for doctors to manage their schedules, and for administrators to oversee the entire process.
